# Privacy Policy for Bill Calculator **Effective date:** June 30, 2025 **1. Introduction** Bill Calculator (“we”, “our”, or “us”) helps you calculate final prices with service charges and VAT. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. **2. Information We Collect** - **Local Data:** Your bill entries (base price, service %, VAT %), optional notes, and timestamps are stored locally on your device only. - **Advertising Data:** We display ads via Google Mobile Ads SDK, which may collect your device’s advertising ID. We do not collect or store any personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers. **3. How We Use Your Data** - Local data is used only to populate your history screen and enable note-taking and sharing features. - Advertising data is processed by the ad network and is not sent to our servers. **4. Data Sharing** We do **not** share your personal data with third parties, except with Google’s ad network as required for serving ads. **5. Data Security** All your data is stored on your Android device using the SharedPreferences API within the app’s private sandbox. **6.
